Definition 1.1.1. Associative algebra.

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring with unity \(1_R\text{.}\) An \(R\)-algebra or algebra over \(R\) is a unital right \(R\)-module \(A\) together with \(R\)-bilinear map \(A\times A\to A\) denoted by \((x,y)\mapsto xy\) that is associative, i.e., \((xy)z=x(yz)\) for all \(x,y,z\in A\text{,}\) and there is a unity \(1_A\in A\) such that \(1_Rx=x1_R=x\) for all \(x\in A\text{.}\)
