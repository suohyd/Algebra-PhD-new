Lemma 3.4.8.

Let \(M\) be a right \(A\)-module with the following property.
\begin{equation} \text{For any submodule }N\leq M\text{ there exists a submodule }N'\leq M\text{ such that }M=N\oplus N'\tag{3.4.1} \end{equation}
Then, for any submodule \(L\leq M\) has the above property (3.4.1), i.e., for any submodule \(P\leq L\) there exists \(Q\leq L\) such that \(L=P\oplus Q\text{.}\)
